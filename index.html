§ 07 / FAQ

Questions you'd actually ask.

Is this a real product I can buy today? +

No, not yet. Right now this is a concept being pressure-tested by someone who has lived inside the legacy HSE stack for two decades. The early access list is for operators and field hands who want to weigh in on what gets built and see it as it ships.

Why should I care if you haven't built it yet? +

If you've felt the same frustration with KPA, Veriforce, or paper JSAs that I have, the early access list is your shot at shaping a tool built around your actual workflow rather than the office's idea of it. No commitment, no spam, just build updates.

Will it integrate with ISN, Avetta, Veriforce? +

That's the plan. Training records, MSQ data, and incident metrics will export in formats those portals expect. The intent is to feed the contractor management portal you're already required to use, not replace it.

What about data security? +

Encrypted at rest and in transit, hosted on AWS with row-level tenant isolation. SOC 2 is on the roadmap once there's a paying customer to underwrite it. No promises before that's real.

What about offline use in dead zones? +

Offline-first is a non-negotiable design principle. Forms, signatures, and photos captured locally, queued, synced on reconnect with conflict resolution. This is the default, not a premium tier.
